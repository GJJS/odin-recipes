Berry Smoothie Bowl

Description

Start your day with this vibrant and nutritious smoothie bowl packed with antioxidant-rich berries and customizable toppings. A healthy breakfast that tastes like dessert!

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Honey to taste
  • Granola
  • Chia seeds
  • Fresh fruit for topping
      • Steps
  • Blend frozen berries and banana
  • Add yogurt and blend until smooth
  • Add honey if needed
  • Pour into a bowl
  • Top with fresh fruit
  • Sprinkle granola and chia seeds
  • Serve immediately
