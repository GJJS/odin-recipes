Spicy eggs

Description

Spicy scrambled eggs for breakfast. This is a quick, easy and customisable recipe for those days when simple scrambled eggs is just not enough

    Ingredients
  • 2 Eggs
  • salt
  • Spicy sauce
  • Some butter
  • Pepper
  • Odorless cooking oil
      • Steps
  • Put some oil on the pan and let it heat
  • Put some butter in the pan and reduce heat to medium
  • Crack your eggs and put them in the pan
  • Stir and do not let your eggs cook to much
  • Take your pan out of heat and reduce heat to low
  • Add the butter that you left out and season to taste
  • Put the pan on the heat, finish cooking and serve
